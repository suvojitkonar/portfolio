title: On-call and incident learnings
excerpt: Short write-up of habits that reduced mean time to recovery and improved handoffs between shifts.
publishedAt: "2023"
tags:
  - DevOps
  - Culture
---

Good incidents start with a single incident commander and a shared timeline. We stopped debating in side channels and kept updates in one thread so the next shift could replay decisions quickly.

Blameless reviews focused on systems and process: what signal we missed, what runbook was wrong, and what guardrail would have prevented recurrence. Action items had owners and dates; we tracked them like any other sprint work.

Handoffs improved when we standardized a short written summary: current state, suspected cause, tried mitigations, and open risks. Voice calls alone did not survive timezone changes.
