## Overview

Architected, developed and deployed a production-grade AI agent orchestration platform leveraging the Google ADK and Agent-to-Agent (A2A) protocol. Engineered the backend to support seamless multi-agent communication, ensuring reliable state management and scalable task delegation.

## What I focused on

- Faut tolerant and context aware system
- Leveraging google adk to its full potential
- Avoiding "AI" pitfalls, for e.g - Agents Hallucinations
- Low latency system

## Outcome

Teams could use this orchestration platform and forget about manually selecting agents for their specific needs
